Eclairs

Description

An easy and quick eclairs.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sifted brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix brown sugar, granulated sugar, and butter together in a large bowl.
  2. Mix heavy cream and whipping cream together in a separate bowl.
  3. Add vanilla and beat until smooth.
  4. Add egg yolks and beat until incorporated.
  5. Add cream mixture and beat until light and fluffy.
  6. Stir in brown sugar mixture and beat until light and fluffy.
  7. Serve immediately.
